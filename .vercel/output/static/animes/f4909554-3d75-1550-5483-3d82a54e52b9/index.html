Bungo and Alchemist: Gears of Judgement PG-13

Bungo and Alchemist: Gears of Judgement is a continuation of the Bungo and Alchemist series, where players and viewers engage with famous Japanese literary authors who have been brought back to life to fight mysterious threats to literature. This season deepens the story with new challenges and revelations.
